This dataset is modeled after standard actuarial and health insurance datasets (similar to the ones used in machine learning workshops). It contains 1,338 records and 7 features that influence medical insurance premiums.Dataset Overviewage: The age of the primary beneficiary (ranging from 18 to 64).sex: Gender of the insurance contractor (male/female).bmi: Body Mass Index, providing an understanding of body weight relative to height ($kg/m^2$).children: Number of dependents covered by health insurance.smoker: Smoking status (yes/no), a significant factor in premium calculation.region: The beneficiary's residential area in the US (northeast, southeast, southwest, northwest).charges: Individual medical costs billed by health insurance (Target Variable).
